title: "Use Product Groups"
description: "Step-by-step guide to organising products with product groups for easier filtering and management."
sidebarTitle: "Product Groups"
---

Product groups help you categorise products with similar features, making it easier to manage, find, and track them in large quantities.

<Steps>
<Step title="Access the products screen">
Navigate to the **Products** section using the left-hand menu.
</Step>
<Step title="Assign a product to a group">
Select the product you want to assign. Go to the **Groups** tab, choose the group from the dropdown, and click **Assign Group**.
</Step>
<Step title="Filter products by group">
Open the **Column Manager** by clicking the settings button in the top-right corner of the product screen. Select **Groups** from the dropdown and click **Ok**. The Groups column will now appear in the data grid, allowing you to filter and search for products by group.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="What is a product group?">
A product group is a collection of products that share similar characteristics, used to organise and manage them efficiently.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Can I assign a product to multiple groups?">
Yes, you can assign a product to multiple groups depending on how you want to categorise them.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="How do groups make product management easier?">
Groups let you categorise and filter products based on shared characteristics, making them easier to find and track.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
