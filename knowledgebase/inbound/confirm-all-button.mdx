## Confirm All Button

The **Confirm All** button lets you confirm all tasks on a tab simultaneously instead of clicking each line individually. It works for both inbound receipts (receive and putaway) and outbound pick lists (pick and dispatch).

### Where You Can Use Confirm All

- **Goods In Receipts** — confirm all receive tasks and putaway tasks
- **Goods Out Pick Lists** — confirm all pick tasks and dispatch tasks

### Confirming Goods In

<Steps>
  <Step title="Confirm all receive tasks">
    From the **Home Menu**, go to **Goods In** > **Receipts**. Create or open a receipt, add your products, and click **Receive**. All products move to the **Receive** tab. Click **Confirm All Receive Tasks** to confirm every line at once.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm all putaway tasks">
    Switch to the **Put Away** tab. Click **Confirm All Put Away Tasks** to confirm all lines at once.

    <Warning>Remember to click **Save** after confirming all putaway tasks.</Warning>
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Confirming Goods Out

<Steps>
  <Step title="Confirm all pick tasks">
    From the **Home Menu**, go to **Goods Out** > **Sales Order**. Create or open a sales order and select **Save and Process Picks**. Then go to **Goods Out** > **Picking Lists**, select the relevant pick list, and click **Confirm All Pick Tasks** in the **Pick** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm all dispatch tasks">
    Switch to the **Dispatch** tab and click **Confirm All Dispatch Tasks** to confirm all dispatch lines at once.

    <Warning>Remember to click **Save** to complete the process.</Warning>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Accordion title="Frequently asked questions">
  **Can I still confirm tasks individually?**

  Yes. The Confirm All button is a shortcut, but you can still confirm tasks one at a time.

  **What if I make a mistake after confirming all tasks?**

  You can **unconfirm tasks individually** and make adjustments before finalising.

  **Do I need to save after using Confirm All?**

  Yes. Always click **Save** after confirming tasks to finalise the process.
</Accordion>
