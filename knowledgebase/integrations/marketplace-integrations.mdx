Clarus WMS integrates with a range of e-commerce and marketplace platforms, allowing sales orders to flow directly into the warehouse without manual entry. Common channels include **Shopify**, **eBay**, and **Amazon**.

## How a marketplace integration works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Order creation">
    Orders placed on the marketplace are automatically imported into Clarus for fulfilment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Warehouse processing">
    Orders are picked, packed, and dispatched within Clarus.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Status updates">
    Dispatch confirmations, tracking references, and fulfilment statuses are returned to the marketplace, so customers receive accurate delivery information.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Benefits

- Reduced manual data entry
- Faster order processing
- Improved order accuracy
- Automated tracking updates
- Greater visibility across the fulfilment process

<Info>The exact functionality depends on the marketplace platform and your operational requirements. Your implementation consultant can advise on the most suitable configuration during onboarding.</Info>

<Tip>Need to connect a channel that isn't listed? Talk to your implementation consultant — additional platforms and bespoke connectors can often be accommodated.</Tip>
