## Understanding Pick Rules

Pick rules determine how stock is prioritised during the picking process. Choose the rule that best matches your business needs for efficient and accurate stock dispatch.

### Available Pick Rules

| Pick Rule | How It Works | Best For |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **FIFO** (First In, First Out) | Picks the oldest stock first | Perishable goods; ensuring older stock is dispatched first |
| **LIFO** (Last In, First Out) | Picks the most recent stock first | Products where newest stock should be prioritised |
| **Sell By Date** | Picks items closest to their sell-by date first | Products with expiration dates |
| **Best Before Date** | Picks items closest to their best-before date first | Products where freshness matters, even without strict expiry |

<Tip>Pick rules are applied at the account level, meaning you can customise them for different accounts. You can change the pick rule at any time through the account settings.</Tip>
