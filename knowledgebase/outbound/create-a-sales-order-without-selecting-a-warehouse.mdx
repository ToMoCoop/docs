## Creating Sales Orders Without a Specific Warehouse

When you have stock across several warehouses and the order can be fulfilled from any location, you can create a sales order without pre-selecting a warehouse.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start a sales order">
    Begin creating your sales order as usual.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the warehouse field">
    Notice that the warehouse field is automatically populated with a default value.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remove the selected warehouse">
    Click the cross ("X") icon inside the warehouse field to remove the pre-selected warehouse.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the sales order">
    Once the warehouse is removed, the system will automatically pick stock following its pre-set sequence without prioritising any particular warehouse.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>If you do not remove the warehouse, the system will only pick stock from the pre-selected warehouse. The system follows the pre-set stock allocation sequence to determine which warehouse to pick from.</Info>

<Tip>Currently, you must manually remove the warehouse selection each time. There is no default setting to skip warehouse selection automatically.</Tip>
