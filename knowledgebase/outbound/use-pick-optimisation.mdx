## Pick Optimisation

Pick optimisation improves how stock is picked based on your chosen pick rule. There are two strategies to choose from.

### Storage Unit Breakdown Avoidance

Prioritises picking full storage units while still following your chosen pick rule. This reduces handling time and minimises partial picks.

**Best for:** High-volume orders where speed is crucial, as it avoids splitting storage units.

### Stock Rotation

Ensures picks strictly follow your chosen pick rule, even if it means breaking down storage units. This guarantees accurate stock rotation.

**Best for:** Regulated products or situations where strict stock rotation is mandatory.

<Warning>You can only choose one optimisation strategy at a time -- either **Storage Unit Breakdown Avoidance** or **Stock Rotation**.</Warning>

<Tip>You can change the optimisation strategy at any time by accessing the account settings.</Tip>
