## Image capture overview

You can take photos on your HHD and attach them directly to tasks in **Goods In** or **Goods Out** workflows. This makes it easy to record visual evidence during warehouse operations.

<Warning>Image capture must first be configured within your HHD flow before it can be used.</Warning>

## Using image capture in your workflow

Once the flow is set up, you can use image capture in both Goods In and Goods Out. For example, in the "Receive by Storage Unit" flow:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Scan the storage unit reference">
    Scan the storage unit reference for the pallet you are receiving.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/knowledgebase/image-capture-hhds-1.png" alt="HHD screen for scanning storage unit reference" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Capture Image">
    The system takes you to a screen with a **Capture Image** button. Select it to begin.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/knowledgebase/image-capture-hhds-2.png" alt="HHD screen showing the Capture Image button" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Take a photo">
    The HHD's camera activates, allowing you to take a photo. You will then see the task ID screen, with options for Back, Next, or Capture Image.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/knowledgebase/image-capture-hhds-3.png" alt="HHD task ID screen with Back, Next, and Capture Image options" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and confirm">
    After taking a photo, you have three options:

    - **Delete** the image
    - **Retake** the image
    - **Confirm** the image

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/knowledgebase/image-capture-hhds-4.png" alt="HHD image review screen with Delete, Retake, and Confirm options" />
    </Frame>

    Once confirmed, a message appears confirming the image has been saved.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add more photos (optional)">
    If you need additional photos for the same task, select **Take Another Photo**. You can attach **up to 10 images per task ID**.

    When you are finished, press **Next** to continue to the following task. The process repeats for each new task.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/knowledgebase/image-capture-hhds-5.png" alt="HHD screen after completing image capture for a task" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Viewing attached images

To review images from the **desktop app**:

1. Navigate to **Goods In** > **Receipts**.
2. Locate the receipt you processed on the HHD.
3. On each receipt line, you will see a **paperclip icon**.
4. Click the paperclip to view the images attached to that task.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/knowledgebase/image-capture-hhds-6.png" alt="Receipt line showing the paperclip icon for attached images" />
</Frame>

Use the arrows to browse images and the **Download** button to save them to your PC.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/knowledgebase/image-capture-hhds-7.png" alt="Attachments screen with image navigation arrows and Download button" />
</Frame>

## Skipping image capture

If no images are required for a task, simply press **Next** after scanning the storage unit. The task completes without attaching any images.
