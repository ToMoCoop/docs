## Viewing your integrated printers

You can see all printers connected to your Cloud Print integration from the **Settings** menu. This is useful for verifying integrations and finding recently added printers.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Settings menu">
    From the left-hand menu, click **Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Printers">
    Select **Printers** from the settings options. You will see a list of all your current printer integrations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search for new integrations">
    If you cannot find a recently added printer, type its name into the **Integrated** search box. The new integration should then appear in the list.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>You can also manage individual printer settings directly from the **Printers** list.</Tip>

<Accordion title="FAQ">
  **Where can I find my list of printers?**

  Go to **Settings** > **Printers** to view all your integrated printers.

  **What if I cannot see a newly added printer?**

  Try typing its name in the **Integrated** search box.

  **How do I add a new printer to the list?**

  You can add a new printer integration by going to **Settings** > **Integrations** and following the setup steps.
</Accordion>
