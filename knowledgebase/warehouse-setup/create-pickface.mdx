Pickfaces are designated locations used for picking smaller quantities of stock. By configuring locations as pickfaces and associating products, you can streamline replenishment and ensure product availability.

<Steps>
<Step title="Configure the location as a pickface">
Go to the **Locations** screen and select the location you want to use. Open the menu for that location and find the **Is Pickface** option. Enable it to designate the location as a pickface.
</Step>
<Step title="Associate a product with the pickface">
Navigate to the **Products** screen and find the product you want to assign. Scroll down to the **Pickfaces** tab. Choose between two pickface types:

- **Fixed**: You manually assign specific locations to the product
- **Dynamic**: Uses a pre-defined location group, automatically including all locations within that group
</Step>
<Step title="Set triggers and save">
Use the lower tab to either choose specific location(s) for a fixed pickface or a location group for a dynamic pickface. Define the **minimum** and **maximum** stock triggers, then click **Save**. The location or group of locations will now operate as a pickface.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
For dynamic pickfaces to work correctly, ensure that the **Is Pickface** option is enabled for all locations in the chosen group.
</Info>
