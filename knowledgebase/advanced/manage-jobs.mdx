## Managing warehouse jobs

The Jobs screen gives you a centralised view of all warehouse tasks, including picking, packing, and goods out operations. You can customise the view and drill into individual job details.

## Accessing jobs

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Jobs screen">
    Select **Jobs** from the menu bar on the left-hand side. The screen displays all created jobs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customise the view">
    Adjust the columns to show information most relevant to you, such as **Goods Out Reference**, **Pick List**, and **Completed At**. Save your customised view for future use.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Viewing job details

Click **Show** next to any job to see its full details, including status and goods out reference.

The job details screen has two key tabs:

- **Tasks** -- lists all tasks associated with the job, showing which products need to be picked and their quantities.
- **Transactions** -- displays all completed transactions, helping you track progress and completion.

<Accordion title="FAQ">
  **Can I customise the Jobs screen?**

  Yes, you can adjust columns to display the most relevant information and save your view for future use.

  **How do I track completed tasks for a job?**

  Go to the **Transactions** tab to view all completed transactions for the job.
</Accordion>
