## Personalise Your Invoices

Customising your invoices with your company logo and address ensures they look professional and maintain your brand's presence.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add your company logo">
    Upload your company logo through the **Invoice Settings** section. This helps maintain your brand's presence on every invoice.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Include your company address">
    Ensure that your company's address is correctly displayed on the invoice. Update it in the settings if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview the invoice">
    Before sending the invoice to your client, review it to confirm that all customisations are correctly applied.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>Your logo and address will automatically appear on all future invoices once updated in the settings.</Tip>

<Info>Changes to settings apply only to new invoices. Existing saved invoices will not be updated retroactively.</Info>
