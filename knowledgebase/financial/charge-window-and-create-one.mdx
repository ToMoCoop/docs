## What Is a Charge Window?

A charge window defines the time period during which charges are applied to customer accounts. Charges can run on a daily, weekly, or monthly basis.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access charge window settings">
    From the left-hand menu, click **Charges** then **Charge Windows**. This displays all existing charge windows.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a new charge window">
    Click **+Add Charge Window** to begin setup.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter charge window details">
    Fill in the following fields:

    - **Name**: Give the charge window a clear name (e.g., "Weekly Storage Charge").
    - **Frequency**: Choose either **Day** or **Month** from the dropdown.
      - **Day**: Pick specific days of the week (e.g., Sunday to Sunday) or choose all days for daily charges.
      - **Month**: A calendar will appear. Select the specific days within the month when charges should run.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the charge window">
    Click **Save** to activate the charge window.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>After creating the charge window, your next step is to set up a **Charge Card** that defines the actual charges applied during the specified window.</Tip>
