The billing engine supports **pick charge subtypes**, giving you more precision over how warehouse operations are charged. You can apply different rates to full, part, and pickface picks, all automated through your charge cards.

## How it works

The system recognises three distinct pick scenarios:

- **Full**: triggered when the entire contents of a pallet are picked (for example, 10 of 10 items).
- **Part**: applied when only a portion of a pallet's stock is picked (for example, 5 of 10 items).
- **Pickface**: triggered whenever stock is picked from a designated pickface location.

## Applying a subtype

<Steps>
<Step title="Open the charge card">
From the left-hand menu, go to **Charges** then **Charge Card**, and select the relevant charge card.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/knowledgebase/pick-charge-subtypes-1.png" alt="Navigating to Charges then Charge Card" />
</Frame>
</Step>
<Step title="Find the Subtype field">
Scroll down to **Pick Charges** and move to the right to find the new **Subtype** field.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/knowledgebase/pick-charge-subtypes-2.png" alt="The Subtype field on a pick charge line" />
</Frame>
</Step>
<Step title="Assign the subtype">
Assign the desired subtype — **Full**, **Part**, or **Pickface** — to your charge line.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Important considerations

To keep your billing accurate, keep the following in mind:

<Warning>
**Unit of Measure (UoM):** if you set your UoM to **Item**, you lose the ability to charge for full pallet picks separately.
</Warning>

<Note>
**Rate card alignment:** make sure charges are applied against the correct task type — for example, distinguishing pallet pick rates from case or item pick rates — so they stay aligned with your specific rate cards.
</Note>
