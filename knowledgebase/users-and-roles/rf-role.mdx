## Overview

The RF (Radio Frequency) role is designed for warehouse staff who use RF scanners to manage daily operations. It grants full access to all RF scanner functions while restricting access to broader system features like reports or transactions.

## RF user permissions

- **Full access** to all RF scanner functions
- **Picking and packing** via RF scanners
- **Stock adjustments** and movements using RF devices
- **Warehouse operations** management through RF devices

<Warning>The RF role does not grant access to reports, transactions, or system management features. It is limited to RF scanner functions only.</Warning>

## Assigning the RF role

To assign the RF role, select **RF** from the role dropdown when [creating a user](/knowledgebase/users-and-roles/creating-users).

<Info>RF users will have access only to the warehouses assigned to them. You can manage warehouse assignments through user settings.</Info>
