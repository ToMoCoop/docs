## Overview

The Financial role gives users specific access to manage the financial aspects of the system. Users with this role can view and manage all **charges** related to orders, shipments, and other transactions.

<Info>The Financial role is focused exclusively on charge management. It does not grant access to stock, reports, accounts, or broader system settings.</Info>

## What Financial users can do

- View and manage all **charges** within the system
- Handle costs and pricing details across operations
- Adjust charges for individual orders

## What Financial users cannot do

- Access stock management or reports
- Create or edit accounts or warehouses
- Process payments (payment processing typically falls outside this role)
