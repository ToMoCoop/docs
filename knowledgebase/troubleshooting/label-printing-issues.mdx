title: "Label Printing Across Multiple Pages"
description: "Diagnose and resolve labels printing across multiple pages with this step-by-step troubleshooting guide."
sidebarTitle: "Label Printing Issues"
---

If your label is printing across two pages, it is usually due to an incorrect template assigned to the account.

## How to fix it

<Steps>
<Step title="Raise a support ticket">
Contact the support team (email, phone, or live chat) and explain your label printing issue.
</Step>
<Step title="Wait for template confirmation">
The support team will find the correct template for your account and activate it. This may take up to 24 hours.
</Step>
<Step title="Assign the new template">
Once the new template is active, assign it to the affected account(s) within your system.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
It can take up to 24 hours for the new template to become active.
</Info>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="What causes the label to print across multiple pages?">
This usually happens when the wrong template is assigned to the account.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Can I fix the template issue myself?">
No, you will need to contact support to ensure the correct template is identified and activated.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="What should I do if the issue persists after updating the template?">
Contact support again to investigate further if the problem continues after the template has been updated.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
