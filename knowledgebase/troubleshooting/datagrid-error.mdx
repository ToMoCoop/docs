Loading delays and "query was not successful" errors typically occur when filtering or searching large datasets. Here is how to resolve them.

## Fixes

<Steps>
<Step title="Refresh the screen">
Refreshing the screen a few times will often resolve the issue and allow the desired information to load.
</Step>
<Step title="Apply a date or time filter">
If refreshing does not work, apply a date or time filter to your search. You do not need to match the exact date -- selecting a smaller range (e.g. the **past week**) will significantly reduce load times and prevent errors by limiting the data processed.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Why do loading delays occur?">
Loading delays typically occur when filtering or searching large datasets, as the system processes a large amount of information.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="What should I do if refreshing the screen doesn't work?">
Apply a date or time filter to your search. This limits the data the system needs to process.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Do I need to select the exact date for the filter?">
No, choosing a smaller range such as the past week is usually sufficient to reduce load times.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
